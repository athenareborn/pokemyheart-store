## Executive Summary

### 2025 Campaign Performance (Reference)
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Spend | $8,251.43 AUD |
| Total Conversions | 397 |
| Average CPA | $20.78 AUD |
| Total Impressions | 815,633 |

| Conversion Rate | 0.66% |

### What Worked
- "I choose you" messaging resonated strongly
- Pokemon + Valentine combination keywords dominated
- Video creative outperformed static images 10:1
- Mobile traffic converted at 91% of all conversions
- Age 25-34 delivered 50% of conversions at best CPA

### What Failed
- Sitelinks with "Limited stock" language → DISAPPROVED
- Age 45+ audience → Poor ROI ($44-53 CPA vs $17 for 25-34)
- Tablet/TV placements → Negligible conversions
- Static images alone → Low engagement

### Policy Compliance
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Trademark (Pokemon) | Use "Pokemon-style" or "Pokemon-inspired", not official branding |
| Misrepresentation | NO fake scarcity, NO fake reviews, NO misleading claims |
| Disapproved ads | Review all copy against Google policies before launch |
| Account suspension | Use completely clean entity, different payment, different IP |

### Circumvention Checklist
- [x] Different business entity (Move It Fast Pty Ltd)
- [x] Different website domain (ultrararelove.com)
- [x] Different platform (Next.js/Stripe, not Shopify)
- [x] Different Google account (new)
- [x] Different GMC account (new)
- [x] Different payment method (new card)
- [x] Different IP/device for setup (mum's phone)
- [x] No cross-linking to old accounts
- [x] No urgency/scarcity language

1. Sitelinks with "Limited stock" - DISAPPROVED
