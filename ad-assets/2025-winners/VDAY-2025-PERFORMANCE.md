## Key Insights

### What Worked
1. **Static ads outperformed video on ROAS** (4.04x vs 2.91x)
2. **But video drove volume** ($2,293 spend â†’ 98 purchases)
3. **Carousel format showed potential** (5.77x ROAS but only $40 test budget)
4. **LiquidLuck UGC video** was the best video performer

### Copy That Worked
The winning static ads used catalog/product imagery with copy focused on:
- "Not just for Pokemon fans"
- "Shiny, thoughtful, romantic"
- "Limited edition"
- Urgency and scarcity

### What to Replicate for 2026
1. Use the LiquidLuck video style (UGC, authentic, TikTok native)
2. Lead with static images in feed (higher ROAS)
3. Test carousel format more aggressively (5.77x ROAS winner)
4. Use "audience expansion" copy ("Don't have to be a fan...")
