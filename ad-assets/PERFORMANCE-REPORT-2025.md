## Copy Analysis

### Top Performing Headlines

1. **"The Perfect Gift for Pokemon Lovers!"**
   - Used on video ads
   - Clear value proposition
   - Targets identity (Pokemon lover)

2. **"I Choose You"**
   - Pokemon reference (Ash's iconic line)
   - Romantic double meaning
   - Works for anniversary + Valentine's

3. **"Tired of the Same Old Valentine's Day Gifts?"**
   - Problem-aware hook
   - Differentiates from generic gifts
   - Appeals to people seeking unique gifts

### Copy Elements That Worked

| Element | Example | Why It Worked |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| Pokemon Reference | "I choose you" | Instant recognition, emotional |
| Identity Targeting | "Pokemon Lovers" | Self-selection, qualified traffic |
| Occasion Specific | "Anniversary", "Valentine's" | Intent + urgency |
| Problem/Solution | "Tired of same old..." | Positions as alternative |
| Gift Framing | "Perfect Gift" | Purchase intent language |

### Primary Text Patterns

**Format that worked:**
```
[Hook with Pokemon reference]
[Occasion mention]
[Product benefit]
[Call to action]
```

**Example:**
> "Looking for the perfect Valentine's gift for the Pokemon fan in your life?
> Our custom Pokemon cards let you say 'I Choose You' in the most memorable way.
> Order now for Valentine's delivery!"
